STA ELENA

Santa Elena is the gateway to Bicolandia and offers exceptional beauty and history. The town displays glimpses of adventure and fun in different tourist spots. The town was named after the church St.Helena the Empressm which is located at the heart of the town proper. Experience one of the great waterfalls of Camarines Norte in the legendary Imee-Irene Twin Falls at Baranggay Bulala. It was named after the two daughters of the Marcoses who allegedly found gold. Revitalize and be one with nature with its cold and refreshing waters. Offering good ambiance and peace, Sta. Elena River Hut and Swing or SERHAS is the newest agri-tourism park. It is set o the riverbanks of Baranggay San Vicente and makes you fell like you are in Bali, Indonesia with its relaxing view of rice fields and the flowing water of the river. It is the perfect spot to swing away negativity, unwind and relax. #STAELENA #StHelenaChurch #TwinFalls
