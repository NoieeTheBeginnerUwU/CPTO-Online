SAN VICENTE

Dubbed as the Town of Discovery and Adventure, San Vicente is a landlocked municipality. Its main tourist draw is the refreshing mananap Falls, which has an approximate height of 20-25 ft. with deep cool waters for swimming, freshwater fishing, camping or simply appreciating nature's beauty. To pay homage to this charming place, one of the most awaited events in their municipality is their promotion of their local culture and tourism through their colorful Mananap Festival. #SANVICENTE #TownOfDiscovery #MananapFalls
