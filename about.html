Camarines Norte, Bicol's northernmost province, is one of the country's unheralded destination which became one of the tourist hotspots in recent years because of its well kept secrets. Cam Norte is 343kms away from Manila. It takes 7-8 hrs of travel by land. It has twelve towns divided into 2 districts 1. Tagalog Speaking *Labo, Sta. Elena, Capalonga, Paracale and Jose Panganiban* 2. Bicol Speaking District * Daet, Mercedes, Vinzons, Basud, San Lorenzo Ruiz, San Vicente and Talisay*. For more info, like our facebook page, Come To Cam Norte.
