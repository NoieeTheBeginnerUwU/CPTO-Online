LABO

The town Labo is geographically located relatively at the center of the province of Camarines Norte. It is approximately 335 kilometers south of Manila and 17 kilometers away from Daet, the capital town of the province. Labo being located at the center of the province and the most populous town, serves as the agricultural center as well as potential center for business, trade, tourism as well as secondary industrial growth center. Labo is a landlocked municipality surrounded with plains and mountains and there's no nearby beach to visit. There's a lot of tourist spots that you can visit here in Labo. You can enjoy trekking with your friends and family at Mt. Cadig and Mt. Bagacay. You can also visit some of the resorts and waterfalls here in Labo. They offer unique experiences for the guests wherein you can dine and relax during your leisure time. #LABO #HeartOfCamarinesNorte #MtCadig #MtBagacay
