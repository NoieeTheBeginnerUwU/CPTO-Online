Talisay

Talisay is the smallest municipalit of Camarines Norte, constituting only 15 barangays - with three barangays cuddled by the Pacific Ocean. Blessed with a vast agricultural area, agriculture is the economic backbone of the town, primarily rice and coconut farming. Talisay is recognized as the rice granary of Camarines Norte, which became the origin of "Paruyan Festival" that is celebrated annually by Talisayons. Having three coastal barangays with promising coastlines and engaging waves for intermediate surfers, Talisay is starting to be recognized now as a Surfing Hub in Camarines Norte. The municipality is also blessed with 20.5 hectares of mangrove forest that offers tourists ecoadventure experiences, a taste of the first and authentic Tuhoy Recipes in Camarines Norte and unique Tuhoy Harvesting Experience. #TALISAY #RiceGranaryOfCamNorte #FestivalOfLights
