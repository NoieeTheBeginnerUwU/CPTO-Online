CAPALONGA

Capalonga, which is 73 kilometers northeast of Daet, takes pride in its undescovered laid back charm. Because of its coastal location, it abounds with marshes, mangroves, pristine islands, and resorts that are dotted on its shores. The town us also home to the Black Nazarene, which is believed to be as powerful as its counterpart in Quiapo. Every first Friday of the month, especially on the first Friday of May, tourists especiallu Chinese businessmen flock to Capalonga to pay homage and seek petitions from the popular icon. #CAPALONGA #CapalongaHomeOfTheBlackNazarene
